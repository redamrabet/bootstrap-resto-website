Brussels

The Brussels Pizza YOLO restaurant is located next to the communal house (Hôtel Communal)
of Ixelles (former residence of Berlioz) at the intersection of the rue du Conseil and rue du Collège, between Place Flagey and Porte de Namur.

Open Monday to Friday from 07:00 to 01:00, Saturday from 10:00 to 01h00.
Close on Sunday.
Rue de la Tulipe, 41 - 1050 Brussels - Tel. 02.515.63.85 - Fax 02.515.63.97
